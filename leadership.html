Leadership

The leadership hierachy in Tum Catholic Community comprises of the following:

CHAPLAIN

Father Richard

He is in charge of spiritual growth of the members
He is also in charge of guidance and counseling.

PATRON

Dr Egesa

He Links the C.C with the administration.
He Coordinates and supervise the C.C annual elections.
He is the Catholic Community mentor.

Main Office

Chairperson

Mike Ogutu

The Chairperson is responsible of Chairing all C.C meetings, makes the final decision in case of controversial discussions; in meetings and other activities he/she gives the final word, is a signatory to C.C transactions, Ensures that all officials and committees’ function effectively and Link the community with the alumni.

Vice chairperson

Lindsey Omotto

The Vice Chairperson Works hand in hand with the chairperson in line with the duties of the Chairperson.
Represents the chairperson in his/her absentia.
Is the custodian of the community’s discipline
Is in charge of welfare

Secretary

Georgina Muthoni

The Secretary Writes the minutes in any meeting and retrieve if need arises, Chairs meetings in the absence of the chairperson and vice chairperson, Is in charge of any incoming and outgoing correspondence in consultation with C.C officials, Is a signatory to C.C transactions, Is in charge of production of membership card and forwarding to SCC coordinator for distribution, Keeps a record of all other members’ details, Prints any communication, Is in charge of information distribution of events to members and public.

Vice secretary

Lavender Khasandi

The Vice Secretary Works hand in hand with the secretary
He/She Assumes the duties of the Secretary in the absence of the secretary
Manages the office stationery

Organizing secretary

Xavier Alaba

The Organizing Secretary Organizes all the C.C events in collaboration with the office
Keeps the officials and members informed about the meetings and giving information concerning the meeting
Ensures there is an MC in events and becomes MC by default in any case

Treasurer

Benedict Opiyo

The Secretary receives and banks the community’s finances
Keeps the financial records of the community.
Informs the officials and the members about the community’s financial status.
Is a signatory to C.C transactions
Reads the financial report at the end of semester.

SCC Leader

Chris Mutinda

The SCC have a committee comprising of all the heads of the SCCs.
Ensures full participation of participation of SCCS in community activities
Is in charge and oversee all activities involving the SCCs
Is in charge of determination of SCC membership and keep record of the same.

Liturgical Leader

Emmanuel Bahati

The Litergical Leader have a committee consisting of 3 other members of preferably both genders. The three should organize for morning glory, lunch hour and evening prayers
Is in charge of liturgy, In conjunction with SCC coordinator members organize alter
Is responsible for the spiritual growth of members
Acts as the link between choir and main office

Library and Asset Manager

Hilary Biwott

The Asset manager have a committee of at least 3 members of preferably both gender
He/She Keeps proper records of C.C equipment and ensuring their maintenance
Is in charge of C.C entertainment
Is in charge of C.C inventories transactions.
With the approval of the office come up with ways of acquiring and replacing equipment in their docket

Hospitality Manager

Evans Shivachi

The Hospitality Manager have a committee of at least 3 members of preferably both gender
She/He is in charge of meals and cutlery
Is in charge of hygiene
With the approval of the office stock necessary utensils and office decorations.

Choir Office

Chairperson

Sylvester Nyamai

The Choir Chairperson is in charge of the choir and all the related groups
Chairs all the meetings of the choir office
Is in charge of all activities of the choir as might be decided by the main office and choir office.

Secretary

Updates the records of the choir members and files in coordination with voice reps
Chair meetings in absence of the chairperson

TREASURER

Keeps a record of all financial statements and avail them where need arises.

Caterer/Cateress

Keeps a record of all financial statements and avail them where need arises.

Choir Director
