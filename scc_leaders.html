SCC Leaders

Tum Catholic Community comprises of 8 (eight) Families with 3 (three) leaders per Jumuiya:

Chairperson

The Chairperson is responsible of Chairing all C.C meetings
Makes the final decision in case of controversial discussions in meetings and other activities, he/she gives the final word
Ensures that all officials and committees’ function effectively and Link the community with the alumni.
Is a signatory to Tum Catholic Comunity transactions.

Project Manager

The Vice Chairperson Works hand in hand with the chairperson in line with the duties of the Chairperson.
Represents the chairperson in his/her absentia.
Is the custodian of the community’s discipline
Is in charge of welfare

Secretary

The Vice Chairperson Works hand in hand with the chairperson in line with the duties of the Chairperson.
Represents the chairperson in his/her absentia.
Is the custodian of the community’s discipline
Is in charge of welfare
